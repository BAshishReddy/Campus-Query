SHEKAR KAMMULA

Sekhar Kammula is a prominent figure in Indian cinema, particularly in Telugu cinema. He is acclaimed for his directorial prowess, having garnered two Filmfare Awards South and six state Nandi Awards for his directional works. Kammula's journey began with the independent film Dollar Dreams (2000), which earned him the National Film Award for Best First film of a director. He holds Master of Science and Master of Fine Arts degrees from Rutgers University and Howard University, respectively. Notable among his critically and commercially successful Telugu films are Anand (2004), Godavari (2006), Happy Days (2007), Leader (2010), Fidaa (2017), and Love Story (2021).

KOMATIREDDY VENKAT REDDY

Komatireddy Venkat Reddy (born 23 May 1965) is an Indian politician from Telangana. He was a Member of Parliament of Bhuvanagiri Parliament Constituency from 2019. He is the star campaigner of Telangana Pradesh Congress Committee from 2022. He was Deputy Floor Leader, Telangana Congress Legislative Party and was elected again as MLA of Nalgonda Assembly constituency in 2023. He was was born on 23 May 1965 as the 8th of the nine children of a farmer, Papi Reddy in Brahmana Vellemla, Nalgonda district, Telangana. Komatireddy Venkat Reddy attended Amarajeevi Potti Sreeramulu High School, Malakpet, Hyderabad in 1980 for his SSC. He is a notable alumni of Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology, Hyderabad in 1986 where he pursued Bachelors of Civil Engineering (BE).
